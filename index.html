Strategy

OBJECTIVE: To reduce costs, improve and increase customer engagement.

OP1: Digitization of Processes

-Identify manual business processes/task & automate them.

-Implement sustainable initiatives.

-Transform payment system.

OP2: Development of Mobile Apps

-Identify areas fit for implementation of mobile apps where required

-Deploy apps to enable staff perform critical high impact tasks.

-Deploy apps to report critical information to the G-suite.

OP3: Automation using bots

-Identify and implement Q&A chat bots in applicable platforms.

-Identify talents and build skills around bot development platforms.

-Develop bot framework.

OP4: Driving Digital Cultural Change

-Develop and implement targeted digital awareness campaigns.

-Empower business users.

-Implement crowdsourcing mechanism.

OP5: Maximize Digital Natives

-Reward ideation.

-Drive competition.

-Promote coaching and mentorship.

Benefits

Benefits of AI Chatbots.
Proficient Feedback Collection
Improved customer engagement
24/7 Real-time customer Support
Reduced costs
Answer FAQ , basic and simple queries
Increase staff productivity
Provide self-service for employees
Monitoring Stakeholders/Consumer Data & Gaining Insights
Help build an AI-powered system

Deliverables

Proposed expected deliverables by the team:

  • Conversational Prototype Chatbots

  • Propose a Bot Framework for the Bank

  • Recommend most suitable bot platform to be adopted by the Bank

  • Propose additional use cases for AI Bots
